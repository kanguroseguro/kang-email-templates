# Kanguro Insurance Brand Guidelines

**Version:** 2024
**Designed by:** Sharon T Sarmiento

## Brand Purpose

Kanguro Insurance provides peace of mind to pet owners and renters. This brand manual ensures consistency, clarity, and strength in every expression of the brand across digital channels, physical materials, and day-to-day communication.

Whether in digital channels, physical materials, or day-to-day communication, every detail contributes to building a brand that feels trustworthy, close, and protective. This guide reflects that purpose by establishing clear guidelines on how to communicate our identity across all touchpoints: from our tone of voice and logo usage to color palettes and visual assets.

This manual is a living document that will continue to grow as our brand evolves. We encourage every team member, partner, and collaborator to embrace it and use it as a daily reference.

## Color Palette

Kanguro uses a carefully curated color palette to maintain brand consistency across all communications.

### Primary Colors

| Color | Hex Code | Usage |
|-------|----------|-------|
| **Coral Red** | `#F87872` | 60% - Primary brand color |
| **Coral Pink** | `#FFB3A8` | 30% - Secondary accent |
| **Navy Blue** | `#002454` | 30% - Text and contrast |
| **Sky Blue** | `#CEEDFF` | 10% - Light accent |
| **Cream** | `#F5F2ED` | 10% - Background |

### Color Usage Guidelines

- **Primary color (Coral Red)** should preferably be the main color used in the brand's colorimetry
- All colors must maintain the identity established in the brand's colorimetry
- Maintain consistent iconography throughout all communications for consistent visual identity
- Use a **single solid tone** - preferably the primary color
- Avoid excessive use of fills. If necessary, apply a fill with a maximum of 20% opacity, allowing for a clean, minimal look

## Typography

### Font Family: Poppins

Poppins is the primary typeface for all Kanguro communications.

#### Heading 1 (Título 1)
- **Weights:** Black, ExtraBold, Bold
- **Alternative:** Negrita/Italica
- **Use:** Main headlines and prominent titles

#### Subheading (SubTítulo 1)
- **Weights:** Bold, SemiBold, Medium
- **Alternative:** Negrita/Italica
- **Use:** Section headers and subheadings

#### Body Text (Texto)
- **Weights:** Regular, Light, ExtraLight
- **Alternative:** Negrita/Italica
- **Use:** Body copy and paragraph text

### Typography Rules

- **DO NOT USE CAPS** (No utilizar MAYUSCULAS)
- Use sentence case for all text
- Maintain hierarchy through weight variation, not size extremes

## External Brands

### Partner Brand Guidelines

When collaborating with external logos or brands, the following guidelines must be followed:

#### Color Usage
- External brand logos must be displayed **exclusively in solid black or white**, depending on the design context
- Display guarantees that there is no interaction with Kanguro's colorimetry that could interfere with the brand

#### Minimalist Style
- External brand logos should be **flat, without shadows**
- Maintain a minimalist and neutral approach
- This maintains a professional focus beyond the necessity of identification

#### Design Location
- Logos should be **clearly centred**, preferably in the primary available design space
- Size and proportion should be controlled to avoid logos occupying disproportionate space that draws excessive attention

#### Size and Controlled Proportion
- Ensure that external brand logos do not occupy disproportionate space
- They should be perceived as informative references and not as interactive or decorative elements

### Example: Trustpilot

When displaying Trustpilot reviews or ratings, use only the Trustpilot logo in solid black or white according to these guidelines.

## Audiovisual Guidelines

### Photography Style

#### Shot Types

- **Plano Americano** (American Shot) - Medium-wide framing
- **Plano Medio** (Medium Shot) - Standard medium framing showing subject from waist up
- **Plano Medio Corto** (Medium Close-Up) - Tighter framing from chest up

#### Photography Rules

- **Filter:** CANCUT / BADUNUNY 5%
- **DO NOT** use iconography, brand agent, or custom brand colorimetry
- **DO NOT** use effects that alter the brand's colorimetry (REFER TO COLORIMETRY SECTION)

#### Guidelines for Minimal, Professional Design

These adjustments help achieve a minimalist, professional design that aligns with your brand identity.

## Tone of Voice

Kanguro's communication is like a good friend: **close, clear, and always present** when most needed.

### Communication Principles

**Human, Reliable, and Creative:**
- We speak authentically and without complications
- We believe insurance doesn't have to be confusing or overwhelming
- Our voice is human, reliable, and creative
- Designed so each client feels we're truly on their side

**Direct, Honest, and Straightforward:**
- Every message breaks conventional patterns
- Shows that insurance can be easy, accessible, and above all, about improving our clients' lives
- With Kanguro, the conversation is always direct, honest, and straightforward

### Voice Characteristics

- **Cercana** (Close/Approachable) - Speak like a friend
- **Clara** (Clear) - No confusing jargon
- **Siempre presente** (Always present) - Available when needed
- **Humana** (Human) - Authentic and relatable
- **Confiable** (Reliable) - Trustworthy
- **Creativa** (Creative) - Fresh and engaging
